Case Closed

Case Closed, also known as Detective Conan, is a captivating Japanese anime and manga series created by Gosho Aoyama. The story follows Shinichi Kudo, a brilliant high school detective who is transformed into a child after ingesting a mysterious poison. Under the alias Conan Edogawa, he continues to solve intricate cases while concealing his true identity from those around him. The series masterfully combines elements of mystery, suspense, and drama, keeping viewers on the edge of their seats with its clever plot twists and compelling character arcs.
